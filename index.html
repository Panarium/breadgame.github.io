The Official Guide to Breadgame: A Breadpunk TTRPG

Introduction

Breadgame is a breadpunk tabletop roleplaying game about exploring a world changed by the discovery and activation of countless yeast strains with special properties.

By the time the year 2000 came around, a practical use was found for yeast in nearly every industry. The world as we know it is in a state of unending change as yeast reshapes the path of technology and creatures called Leavens tear through civilizations and virtual spaces for their own means. You play as a Baker, giving you the ability to make a huge impact in the world. Will you put an end to the state of chaos in our world, or will you choose to add to it?
